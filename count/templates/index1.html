Vegetation Management

Power line which passes through the forest frequently causes FOREST FIRE and create a problem of power supply so to save from such condition we are monitoring the hight of the tree near to wire and categories it into 3 part. The first category is Danger which is very near to wire and it is necessary to cut it immediately and the second category is Waring which mean these trees should be cut within 1 year and the third one is Safe category if the hight of the tree is very small.
